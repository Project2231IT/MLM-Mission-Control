## How to Read This

The compliance standard is applied as a **trailing 3-month sales average** compared to a **multiplier of the vendor's monthly rent**.

- **1x** = Must average at least their rent amount per month (current standard, essentially)
- **2x** = Must average 2× rent per month
- **3x** = Must average 3× rent per month
- **4x** = Must average 4× rent per month
- **5x** = Must average 5× rent per month
